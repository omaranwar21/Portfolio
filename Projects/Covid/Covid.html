Spread of COVID-19 in Egypt

Research information

  • Category: Research
  • Field: Mathematical Modeling
  • Submission date: 4 Jan, 2022
  • Video URL: Spread of Covid-19 in Egypt (SEIQRDP)

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19), is a potentially deadly illness. It affects the respiratory system and spreads from one person to another. The complexity of designing effective measures of epidemic control has increased as governments face the challenge of protecting the public while allowing economic activity. The goal of this study was to apply a modified Susceptible-Exposed-Infected Quarantined-Recovered-Death-Insusceptible (SEIQRDP) by a compartmental mathematical model for prediction of COVID-19 epidemic dynamics incorporating pathogen in Egypt.
